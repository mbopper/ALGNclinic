Our services.

Integrated chiropractic and massage services in Loughton, Essex — each designed to address the cause, not just the symptom.

01

Chiropractic Care.

Our chiropractic care is evidence-based and tailored to your presentation. We use spinal manipulation, joint mobilisation, and soft tissue techniques to restore movement, reduce pain, and improve function — all grounded in current clinical research.

We treat a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions including lower back pain, neck pain, sciatica, headaches, joint stiffness, and disc-related issues. Every treatment plan begins with a full assessment so we understand not just where the pain is, but why it's there.

Chiropractic at ALGN is never passive. We combine manual therapy with progressive exercise rehabilitation so you leave each session with tools to support your recovery between appointments. Our aim is always a clear discharge plan — not indefinite treatment.

All ALGN chiropractors are registered with the General Chiropractic Council (GCC), the UK's statutory regulatory body for chiropractic.

02

Sports Massage.

Sports massage at ALGN is for anyone who moves — not just competitive athletes. Whether you train regularly, play recreational sport, or have a physically demanding job, sports massage helps manage the load your body carries day to day.

Our therapists use a range of soft tissue techniques including effleurage, petrissage, friction, and trigger point therapy to reduce muscle tension, improve circulation, and support tissue recovery after training or injury.

Sports massage at ALGN is informed by your training history and goals. We don't deliver generic treatments — we work with the same level of clinical reasoning as our chiropractic sessions. If you're recovering from injury, we coordinate your massage with your wider treatment plan.

Available as a standalone service or as part of an integrated treatment programme alongside chiropractic care. Patients from Loughton, Buckhurst Hill, Chigwell, and across Essex are welcome.

03

Deep Tissue Massage.

Deep tissue massage targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue to address chronic tension patterns, myofascial restrictions, and areas of accumulated tightness that don't respond to lighter treatment.

This modality is particularly effective for postural tension from prolonged sitting or screen-based work — a common presentation among our patients across Essex. It's also beneficial for managing chronic pain conditions, tension headaches, and restricted range of movement.

Our therapists apply sustained, focused pressure to areas of restriction using their hands, forearms, and elbows — working slowly and deliberately to allow tissues to release without unnecessary discomfort. Communication throughout is encouraged so pressure is always appropriate for you.

Deep tissue massage can be delivered as a standalone session or in combination with chiropractic care for patients where both manual therapy and soft tissue work are indicated.

04

Mobility & Recovery.

Mobility and recovery work sits at the intersection of rehabilitation and performance. It's where we take patients who have moved through acute treatment and are ready to rebuild — addressing movement quality, load capacity, and long-term resilience.

Sessions focus on restoring functional range of motion, improving movement patterns, and developing the strength and control needed to stay injury-free. We use progressive exercise protocols, movement screening, and corrective strategies tailored to your specific deficits.

This service is suitable for patients recovering from injury, those preparing for a return to sport, individuals managing chronic or recurring conditions, and anyone who wants to move better and reduce the risk of future problems.

Mobility and recovery is always delivered in the context of your broader health history and treatment goals — not as an isolated add-on but as a structured, evidence-informed programme.
