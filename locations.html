Access

First floor premises with staircase access. Loughton Central line station is a short walk from the clinic.

We are located on the first floor of 236–238 High Road — above the retail units, accessible via the main entrance on the High Road. Look for the ALGN signage. If you have difficulty with stairs or have any access requirements, please email us in advance and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Serving the wider Essex area.

Patients travel to ALGN from across the local area. Our Loughton clinic is well-positioned for the communities below — all within a short drive or public transport journey.

Buckhurst Hill

A short drive north along the A121. Central line accessible via Buckhurst Hill station, one stop from Loughton.

Chigwell

Easily accessible via the A113 or Chigwell Road. Approximately 10 minutes by car from the clinic.

Debden

Neighbouring Loughton directly. Debden Central line station is one stop from Loughton on the Epping branch.

Epping

The terminus of the Central line. Accessible by train to Loughton or a short drive south along the M11 corridor.

Essex & beyond

Patients visit from across Essex and east London. If you're travelling further, the Drive Car Park makes all-day visits straightforward.

Getting here.

By tube

Central Line — Loughton Station

Loughton is on the Epping branch of the Central line. Exit the station and head south along the High Road — the clinic is approximately a 5-minute walk.

By car

Parking on site

The Drive Car Park sits directly adjacent to the clinic on the High Road. Pay and display parking is available throughout the day. Post code for navigation: IG10 1RB.

By bus

High Road stop

Several bus routes serve Loughton High Road, including services from Chigwell, Buckhurst Hill, and Walthamstow. Check TfL or National Express East Anglia for current routes.
